Return and Refund Policy

Effective Date: 24 MAY 2024

Thank you for choosing Watduwant Operations Private Limited for your mobile operator recharge and doctor appointment services. We strive to provide you with excellent service and satisfaction. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding returns and refunds.

Mobile Operator Recharge:

Returns:

Refund Eligibility:

Refunds for mobile operator recharge will only be considered in the event of a failed recharge or if the recharge amount was deducted from your account but the mobile operator did not initiate the recharge.

Notification and Timeframe:

You must notify us of any issues with your recharge within 90 minutes of the transaction date.

Refund Process:

To request a refund, please contact our customer support at Hi@watduwant.com with your transaction details and a description of the issue.

Refunds will be processed within 24 hours of receiving the request.

Doctor Appointment Services:

Cancellations and Refunds:

Cancellation Policy:

You can cancel a doctor appointment without any cost up to 180 minutes before the scheduled appointment.

Refund Eligibility:

Watduwant Operations provide the provision of making Doctor Appointment with no cost at all. As there is no price charged for making any Doctor Appointment, there will be no Refund eligibility

Refunds for doctor appointment services will only be considered if the cancellation is made within the specified time frame.

Refund Process:

To cancel an appointment and request a refund, please log in to your Watduwant account and follow the cancellation process.

General Policies:

Refund Method:

Refunds will be processed using the same method of payment used for the original transaction.

Refund Exceptions:

No refunds will be provided for services that have been successfully delivered, and appointments that are not canceled within the specified time frame.

Contact Information:

For any queries or concerns regarding returns and refunds, please contact our customer support at Hi@watduwant.com.

Watduwant Operations Private Limited reserves the right to amend this policy at any time without prior notice. Please review this policy periodically for any changes.
